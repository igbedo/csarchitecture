Who should apply

This fellowship is designed for professionals ready for architectural depth. Typical applicants have experience in cloud, DevOps, SRE, platform engineering, or security engineering and want to operate at architect level.

  • Cloud / DevOps / SRE engineers (2+ years recommended)
  • Platform engineers seeking architectural authority
  • Security engineers transitioning into cloud security architecture
  • Professionals who can commit 10–15 hours/week

Not for:

  • Entry-level candidates or complete beginners
  • Passive learners or “watch-only” participation
  • Tool-only practitioners with no interest in tradeoffs
  • Applicants unwilling to complete required pre-work

This is not a bootcamp. It is a standards-driven evaluation pathway.

Admissions process

Applicants are evaluated on architectural reasoning, automation proficiency, security integration awareness, operational maturity, and commitment to completion.

1) Written application

Background + architecture thinking questions. We look for clarity, tradeoff reasoning, and seriousness.

2) Technical baseline

A short scenario-based assessment (architecture + automation + investigation). Not multiple choice.

3) Interview (shortlist)

15–20 minutes. Focus on maturity, coachability, and fit for a high-standard cohort.

4) Acceptance + required pre-work

Accepted Fellows complete a mandatory automation + architecture baseline before Week 1. Seats may be forfeited if pre-work is not completed.
